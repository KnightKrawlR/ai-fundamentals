What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Computer systems able to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation.
The study of computer algorithms that improve automatically through experience and by the use of data.
A subset of machine learning using neural networks with multiple layers to analyze various factors of data.
The ability of a computer program to recognize and manipulate human language.
