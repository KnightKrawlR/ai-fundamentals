Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

What's included in the premium subscription?
Our premium subscription includes unlimited access to all learning paths, including advanced topics like Computer Vision and NLP. You'll also get unlimited usage of all AI tools, premium certificates upon completion of learning paths, priority support from our experts, and access to exclusive code repositories and projects.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have premium access until the end of your current billing period. We don't offer refunds for partial subscription periods.
How does the yearly subscription work?
With the yearly subscription, you pay for 12 months upfront and receive a 20% discount compared to the monthly rate. Your subscription will automatically renew after 12 months unless you cancel before the renewal date.
Are the certificates recognized by employers?
Our certificates are recognized by many employers in the tech industry. They demonstrate your practical knowledge and skills in specific AI domains. While they are not accredited university credentials, they show your commitment to professional development and acquiring in-demand skills.
What happens to my progress if I downgrade to a free plan?
If you downgrade to a free plan, your progress on all learning paths is saved. However, you'll only have access to free learning paths. If you decide to upgrade again in the future, you can pick up right where you left off on premium paths.
Can I share my premium account with others?
Premium accounts are for individual use only and should not be shared. If you need accounts for a team or organization, please consider our Enterprise plan, which offers team management features and multi-user access.
